Questions? We Have Answers.

What equipment do I need to get started?

One of the best parts about Woof-Fit is its simplicity. You don't need a gym membership or heavy equipment. We recommend a yoga mat for you, a comfortable harness and leash for your dog (no retractable leashes, please), and some high-value treats. That's it. We designed every workout to be done in your living room, backyard, or local park.

Does my dog need to have prior training?

Not at all. While your dog should know basic cues like "sit" and "stay," Woof-Fit is designed to meet both of you where you are. Our guided workouts naturally reinforce calm, focused behavior. Many members tell us their dog's impulse control and confidence improved dramatically simply by doing the program consistently. This is bonding first, training second.

Is there a free trial available?

Absolutely. We want you to feel the connection before committing. You can start with a full, no-strings-attached free workout video. This gives you and your dog a chance to experience the rhythm, the coaching style, and the joy of training together. No credit card required.

What happens after the free video?

Once you've completed your free workout, you'll receive an invitation to unlock the full Woof-Fit course library. There's zero pressure. The free session is genuinely valuable on its own, but if you're ready to build a consistent, life-changing routine together, the complete series is waiting for you.

What is included in the full course?

The full Woof-Fit series includes a progressive library of follow-along workout videos, each structured around a specific fitness and bonding goal. You'll get warm-ups, strength and mobility flows, cool-downs, and exclusive "park session" bonuses. Every workout is led by Sarit, a Certified Personal Trainer and Certified Canine Fitness Trainer, ensuring both of you are safe and challenged.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this right for me?

Yes, and you're exactly who we built this for. Woof-Fit is not about extreme athleticism; it's about consistent, joyful movement. Modifications are always offered, and the pace is encouraging, never punishing. If you can get down on the floor with your dog, you can do this. This is your premier health and fitness journey, shared with your best friend.

How long is each workout?

Most workouts range between 20 and 35 minutes. We find this is the sweet spot for maintaining both human and canine focus while delivering real fitness benefits. It's long enough to feel accomplished, but short enough to fit into a busy morning or lunch break.

Where can we do the workouts?

Anywhere you have a safe, flat surface. Most members train indoors in their living room or a spare room, but a grassy backyard or a quiet corner of the park works beautifully. As long as you have your phone or tablet to follow along, you've got a health and fitness center wherever you and your dog are together.

Move together. Stay wild. Woof-Fit brings outdoor wellness to you and your pack.
