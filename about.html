The Person Behind
the Program

Woof-Fit was built from a genuine belief that movement strengthens the bond between person and dog. It's not about perfect obedience or gym-level intensity — it's about showing up together, finding your shared rhythm, and discovering that the best workout partner already has four paws and a wagging tail.

Sarit Netanel — Founder & Lead Trainer

Certified Personal Trainer
Certified Canine Fitness Trainer

Sarit Netanel

Sarit Netanel is a Certified Personal Trainer and Certified Canine Fitness Trainer who has spent over a decade blending human movement science with the joyful energy of dogs. Her journey didn’t start in a gym — it started in the park, with her own rescue dog, Leo, who reminded her that the best workouts are the ones you share.

As a premier health and fitness expert, Sarit brings a unique dual certification to every Woof-Fit session. She doesn’t just teach exercises; she designs intentional, safe, and deeply engaging movement experiences where both ends of the leash grow stronger, more confident, and more connected. Her approach is warm, positive, and rooted in the belief that fitness should never feel like a chore — it should feel like quality time with your best friend.

Through Woof-Fit, Sarit is on a mission to help dog-human pairs evolve health and fitness together. She believes that when you train alongside your dog, you’re not just building muscle — you’re building trust, communication, and a bond that lasts a lifetime. Every workout is crafted to meet you where you are, whether you’re a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey.

Why Woof-Fit Exists

Most fitness programs are built for humans. Most dog programs are built for dogs. Woof-Fit is built for both of you — together. Because movement shared isn't just exercise; it's a conversation in trust, a rhythm of confidence, and a moment of pure, focused joy that a standard walk can only hint at.

We believe the bond between a person and their dog is one of the most powerful relationships we can nurture. Woof-Fit transforms that relationship into a ritual — a guided, intentional practice where you both grow stronger, calmer, and more connected, side by side.

Train together. Bond forever. It's not just our tagline — it's the heartbeat of every workout we design, every move we teach, and every story we hear from this incredible community.

— Sarit Netanel, Founder of Woof-Fit
